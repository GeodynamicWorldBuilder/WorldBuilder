Perlin Noise Playground

Interactive preview for the World Builder perlin_noise topography model. Coordinates are entered in km for the model geometry, then converted to meters internally before evaluating the Perlin field. That makes the frequency directly comparable to geodynamic model scales.

Noise parameters

Uses frequency = 10^x. For a 100 km scale model, values near 1e-5 to 1e-4 are often the most informative.
